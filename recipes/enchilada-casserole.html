Enchilada Casserole

Description

"Enchilada" themed casserole, made with beef, cheese, and tortillas.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs. lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 oz.) can tomato paste
  • 1 tsp. ground cumin
  • 2 tsp. chili powder
  • salt to taste
  • 4 (10 in.) flour tortillas
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
