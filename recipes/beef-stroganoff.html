Beef Stroganoff

Description

Beef with sauce, served with noodles or rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. beef chuck roast
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 4 oz. butter
  • 4 green oinions, sliced
  • 4 Tbls. all-purpose flour
  • 1(10.5 oz.) can condensed beef broth
  • 1 tsp. preparded mustard
  • 1 (6 oz.) can sliced mushrooms, drained
  • 1/3 cup sour cream
  • 1/3 cup white wine
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
